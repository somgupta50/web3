Mental health prediction using speech is a growing area of research and development. The basic idea is that the characteristics of a person's speech can be analyzed to identify patterns that may be indicative of various mental health conditions. Some researchers have found that changes in speech patterns can be associated with conditions such as depression, anxiety, bipolar disorder, and schizophrenia.
